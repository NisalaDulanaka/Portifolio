MY PROJECTS

MedicaLink - Online EHR System

June 20, 2024 Web Application Mobile

MedicaLink is an online electronic health information management platform. The longstanding use of paper-based healthcare systems in Sri Lanka has led to issues such as misdiagnoses and delayed patient care. MedicaLink aims to replace this paper-based system by centralizing healthcare information for hospitals across Sri Lanka

Technologies Used

React, ASP .NET Core, MYSQL as the main tech stack.

My Role

As the lead developer of the project I managed the backend, frontend and
the intergration of the two.
